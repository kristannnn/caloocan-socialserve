Caloocan City Seal/Emblem

DESCRIPTION:
- Circular emblem with thick orange outer border
- White ring containing text: "MAKASAYSAYANG LUNGSOD NG CALOOCAN"
- Inner circle split vertically: blue left half, red right half
- Large yellow sun with rays overlapping both halves
- White five-pointed stars scattered on both sides
- Central grayscale Bonifacio Monument
- Bottom: Green laurel branches, "PILIPINAS", and "1962"
